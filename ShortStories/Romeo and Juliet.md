# Rmmeo and Juliet
## Chapter 1
- In the busy city of Verona, there are two important families: the Montagues and the Capulets. They are alway fighting. The fights are so bad that everyone in the city knows about them. In the Motague family, there is a young boy named Romeo. He as string feelings and often acts without thinking. In the Capulet family, there is a beautiful young girl named Juliet. Everyone thinks that is very graceful.
- Romeo and his friends decide to go to a big party at the Capulets' house, even though they are not invited. There, Romeo and Juliet meet and fall in love immediately. They talk, dance, and forget about their family fight for a while. After the party, Romeo cannot stop thinking about Juliet. He goes to see her even though it is dangerous. They decide to secretly get married because they konw their families will not approve. Romeo asks Friar Laurence, a man of the church, to marry them. All is well for a while, but trouble is coming.
## Chapter 2
- Tybalt, Juliet's cousin, is angry with Romeo for coming to the party. He challenges Romeo to a fight. Romeo does not want to fight because he is now Juliet's husband. So, his friend Mercutio fights Tybalt instead and gets killed. In anger, Romeo fights Tybalt and kills him.
- The Prince of Verona is tired of the fights between the two families. He decides to send Romeo away from the city. Romeo has to leave Juliet behind, and he feels very sad. At the same time. Juliet's parents wants her to marry Paris, a man they chose for her. Yet, Juliet loves Romeo and want to be with him. So, she asks Friar Laurence for help again, and the old man makes a plan. She takes a potion that makes her seem dead for a while. She wants Romeo to take her away when she wakes up.
## Chapter 3
- Unfortunately, things do not happen as planned. Romeo hears that Juliet is dead before he gets the message from Friar Laurence. He rushes back to Verona to Juliet's tomb to say goodbye.
- There, he finds Paris who is also sad about Juliet. They fight, and Romeo kills him. Then, thinking that Juliet is really gone, he takes poison and dies next to her. When Juliet wakes up and sees Romeo dead, she is too sad. She tries to kiss the posion from his lips,but it does not work. She cannot live without him. so she takes Romeo's dagger and stabs herself.
## Chapter 4
- The Montagues and Capuleets find Romeo and Juliet dead together. They see what has happened and are deeply sorry for their actions. The families decide to end their fight and build a monument in memory of their children.
- The sad tale of Romeo and Juliet spreads throughout Verona, teaching everyone a lesson on the consequences of hatred and the power of love. The families learn to forgive, and the city becomes a more peaceful place. And so, the story of Romeo and Juliet becomes a legend of love and loss, reminding us all that love should be stronger than hate.
