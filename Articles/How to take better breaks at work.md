# How to take better breaks at work
+ Many people think that working a lot means doing good work. They start work early. They work during weekends and rest little. But, new studies say good breaks are important. That's because happy people often do better work. Even a very short break can help people. Stretching or looking out the window for a few minutes can make people feel better. Using social media can make people feel more tired. One person online says that eating good snacks and drinking water are also important on a break.
