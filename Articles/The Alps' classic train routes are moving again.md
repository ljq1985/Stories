# The Alps' classic train routes are moving again
+ Many people still love traveling by train. It feels special and romantic, and the view are beautiful. Some people even say it's faster than traveling by car. In the Alps, Some of Europe's most famous train routes were closed after floods and landslides. Now, these important lines are open again. The Mont Cenis route between France and Italy has reopened recently. The historic Tauern rainway in Austria has reopened too. New trains now run from cities like Munich, Zurich, and Salzburg. These train journeys are not just fast - they are a special way to explore Europe's beauty.
