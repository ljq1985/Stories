# The cultural significance of a Japanese bag
+ In Japan, many children use a school bag called a randoseru. It's important in Japanese school life. It's a backpack. It's strong and square. It's made of leather. The bag come from the Netherlands a long time ago. Almost every child in school uses it. Parents ask for help online because these bags are expensive. Some families buy used bags to save money.
