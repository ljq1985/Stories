# How to be professional at work
+ It's important to be professional at work. Here are some tips to help you work well with other people. First, be on time. Say what you will do, and do it. Be a person others can count on. Second, keep your desk clean. Third, talking is important. Speak in a nice way. Be kind, but do not talk too much about your personal life. These good habits help everyone feel happy at work. They also help people work well together.
